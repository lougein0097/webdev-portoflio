Content

The purpose of this website is to educate any of those who are unfamiliar with social media and its usefulness in the realm of education, specifically in learning and teaching between teachers and students.

This website will be showcasing the different types of social media tools/ apps to help you teach and learn! The apps and tools that will be showcased are: Pinterest, Reddit, Twitter, YouTube, Slack and Google Classroom.

Each of these tools/ apps play a vital role when it comes to teaching and learning, either by visual, auditory, reading/writing, and kinesthetic learning/ teaching strategies.
